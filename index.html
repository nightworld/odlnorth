The Project

Testing apps and websites on different devices is a pain in the arse.
We're building a free and open test lab with a wide range of devices that anyone can come and use.

Who We Are

Open Device Lab north was setup by NightWorld to help the developer community in the north of the UK. The idea for an open lab was inspired by Andre Jay Meissner and others behind opendevicelab.com.

Who Can Use It

The lab is open to everyone. If you want to test a responsive design or an app your developing then just come along, use any device and our WiFi.
